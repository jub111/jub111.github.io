Romeo Zaccaria

Design, Development

Romeo Zaccaria GmbH is a Berlin-based construction company with italian roots, specialized in the restoration of stucco. I redesigned their outdated website and implemented the new design along with a content management system. The challenge was to create a modern looking design which harmonizes with their existing unique corporate identity.

Sketches

The first step was to create the basic structure and organization. I compared the websites of construction company in order to get an overview over their general layout and their specific norms. In close cooperation with the client I worked on several sketches, which were the foundation for the further desing process.

Restoring the Logo

Before designing the actual website, there was another thing left to do: The clients logo had to be restored, because of its extremely bad quality.

The old and the new Logo

The Final Design

As mentioned early one of biggest challenges was to craft a modern looking design that harmonizes with the clients corporate identity. Based on the sketches, the newly restored logo and their color palette I started to create the look and feel of the new website.
