Romeo Zaccaria is a Berlin-based construction company with Italian roots, specialized in the restoration of stucco. I was responsible for designing their new website along with the front- and back-end development.

Skills

UI/ UX Design
Front- and Back-End Development

Type

Website

Year

2015

The Challenge

The client requested a modern looking website which harmonizes with their existing unique corporate identity. Besides the landing page, the site should also provide individual pages for their services as well as their references. To maintain their website and add additional references, the client asked for an easy-to-use content management system. After considering multiple existing CMS, we agreed on developing a custom solution.

Restoring the Logo

Before I could start to design the actual website, there was another thing left to do: The client's logo had to be restored, because of its poor quality.

Sketches

I compared various websites of construction companies to get an overview of their general layout and their specific norms. In close cooperation with the client, I worked on several sketches, which were the foundation for the further design process.

The Final Design

As mentioned earlier, one of biggest challenges was to craft a modern looking design that harmonizes with the client's corporate identity. Based on the sketches, the newly restored logo, and their color palette I started to create the look and feel of the new website.
